About Ecstasy

Ecstasy is the cultural fest of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar and one of the largest fests in lower Assam and has been attracting thousands of students, artists, and atendees from all over the country. This year, the event is scheduled to take place on the 2nd & 3rd March, and we anticipate an even larger turn out.

Ecstasy 2024 is the annual cultural fest of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar. Experience the joy of art, music, dance, and sports in this two-day extravaganza. Participate in exciting events, learn from experts, and witness amazing performances. Don’t miss this chance to be a part of the Ecstasy wave.
